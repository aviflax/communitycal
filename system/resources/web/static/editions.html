CommunityCal

Editions

Free Edition

  • Unlimited calendars, events, and invitees
  • Seamless integration with calendar apps on phones, tablets, and laptops
  • Email updates to all invitees
  • Web pages:
    • One listing all your calendars, e.g. free.communitycal.app/riverdalehigh
    • And one for each calendar, e.g. free.communitycal.app/riverdalehigh/jv-bball-25-26

Pro Edition (coming soon)

  • Everything in the Free Edition
  • Import invitees from spreadsheets or your address book
  • Collect RSVPs from invitees
  • Send updates via SMS (in addition to email)
  • Send reminders via email and/or SMS
  • Web pages:
    • Use your own domain name, e.g. calendars.riverdalehigh.edu
    • Or use our short domain name, e.g. ccal.app/riverdalehigh
